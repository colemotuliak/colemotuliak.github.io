about me

Hello, my name is Cole Motuliak. I am a 15 year old who loves computers, networking, coding, and technical theatre. I have loved working with computers for as long as I remember, and still do. I have built my own computer, taken many apart, and troubleshooted hardware and software problems. I also am a Microsoft Office Specialist. I currently work at the Rohrman Performing Arts Center as a sound engineer. I do wish in the future to move to working with lighting. When I am not working on school work or at the theatre, you can find me helping out friends and family with computer issues, or sitting in my room browsing the internet, and working with my computer equipment. I also own my own Ubiquiti router, and a Ubiquiti Access Point. I even mounted the access point on my ceiling. One of my projects I am currently working on is wiring my room with ethernet. It will be a big project, but it will be fun. Please check out my social media at the bottom of the page, where I will post pictures of projects I am working on.
